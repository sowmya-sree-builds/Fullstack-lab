About Director

S.Rajamouli (born 10 October 1973) is one of India's most successful and visionary film directors. Known for his grand storytelling, innovative ideas, and spectacular visuals, he has redefined Indian cinemas on a global scale Rajamouli gained fame through movies like Magadheera. Eega, Baahubali: The Beginning. Baahubali: The Conclusion, and the Oscar-winning RRR. His films are celebrated for their emotional depth, powerful characters, and technical brilliance. Rajamouli is admired for blending Indian culture with universal storytelling, making his movies appeal to audiences worldwide. Today, he is regarded as one of the greatest filmmakers in Indian cinema industry

About Music Director



M. M. Keeravani (born July 4, 1961) is a highly acclaimed Indian music composer, singer, and lyricist, known for his work in Telugu, Hindi, and other South Indian films. He gained international fame for composing the song Nattu Nattu" from the film RRR, for which he won an Oscar and a Golden Globe Award. With a career spanning over three decades, Keeravani is celebrated for his musical versatility and has earned numerous awards, including multiple National Film Awards, Nandi Awards, and Filmfare Awards
